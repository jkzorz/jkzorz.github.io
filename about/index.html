About Me

My name is Jackie and I am a PhD Student working in the Energy Bioengineering and Geomicrobiology Group at the University of Calgary. I started working with R and microbial ecology data during my Master's degree, where I was working with hundreds of marine 16S rRNA amplicon sequencing samples. I've compiled some of the code that I've worked with over the years into these tutorials. Hopefully you find them helpful!
