Analyzing Microbial Ecology Data

A place for me to keep track of code I've used to generate R visualizations, and statistical analyses, specifically for the purpose of visualizing and analyzing large microbial ecology datasets.

Disclaimer: I am in no way an R expert, I wouldn't even really consider myself to be at the amateur level. Everything I use has basically worked for me once, and so I keep adapting it over time as needed. There are probably hundreds of better/different ways to do things in R than what I do, but all the code I'm sharing has worked for me and is what I've become relatively comfortable with. I'm also not a stats expert by any means. I rely a lot on some really great resources out there. My go to is Multivariate analyses in microbial ecology
